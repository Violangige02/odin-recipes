Buttermilk pancakes recipe

Short and Sweet

These classic buttermilk pancakes are known for their tender crumb and subtle tang, making them a delicious breakfast or brunch treat.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Egg(s)
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix Dry: Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix Wet: In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg(s), melted butter/oil, and vanilla (if using).
  3. Combine: Gently pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined; a few lumps are okay. Don't overmix
  4. Cook: Heat a lightly greased griddle or pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form and edges look set (about 2-3 minutes). Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Enjoy immediately with your favorite toppings.
