Lasagna Recipe

Classic homemade lasagna recipe

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and plenty of melted mozzarella.

This recipe provides step-by-step instructions to create a truly unforgettable meal that everyone will love. Feel free to customize it with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Sauce

    • Ground meat
    • Onion
    • Garlic
    • Tomatoes
    • Herbs
    • Oil
    • Salt
    • Pepper

  • Cheese

    • Ricotta
    • Egg
    • Permesian
    • Mozarrela

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce: Cook meat (if using) with onion and garlic. Stir in tomatoes and seasonings. Simmer.
  2. Mix the Cheese: Combine ricotta with egg, Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Layer It: In a dish, alternate layers of sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and mozzarella.
  4. Bake It: Cover and bake until bubbly. Uncover and bake until cheese is melted and golden.
  5. Rest: Let it sit for a bit before serving.
